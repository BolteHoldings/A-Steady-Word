Privacy Policy — A Steady Word

Last updated: [DATE]

A Steady Word ("the app") is published by [DEVELOPER NAME]. This policy explains how the app handles information. The short version: the app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information we collect

None. The app does not ask for your name, email, or any account. It does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services, and it contains no third-party SDKs that collect data.

How the app works

A Steady Word runs entirely on your device and works fully offline. All of its content — Scripture passages, reflections, prayers, and quotations — ships inside the app. It does not connect to the internet or to any server we operate.

Data stored on your device

When you save a passage or mark a day of the reading plan complete, that choice is stored locally on your device only, so the app can remember it between sessions. This information never leaves your device, is not visible to us, and is removed if you clear the app's data or uninstall the app.

Permissions

The app does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, or files.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no personal information from anyone, it collects none from children. It is suitable for general audiences.
