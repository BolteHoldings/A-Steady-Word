## Full description (max 4000 characters)

```
A Steady Word is a quiet, unhurried place to steady your heart.

When you're anxious, grieving, weary, waiting, or simply unsure, open the app
and receive a word: a passage of Scripture, a short reflection, and a prayer —
chosen to meet the very thing you're carrying. No feeds, no noise, no ads. Just
one steady word at a time.

The whole app is built on a single, settling truth: that God is sovereign — on
the throne, working all things after the counsel of His own will — and that this
is not a cold doctrine but a deep comfort for ordinary, difficult days.

WHAT'S INSIDE
• 250 encouragements — each pairing a verse (King James Version) with a brief,
  plain-spoken reflection and a prayer.
• "Receive a word" — tell the app what you're facing in your own words, and it
  finds a passage that fits.
• A word for today — one steady reading each day.
• Browse by what you're facing — anxiety, grief, decisions, suffering, waiting,
  loneliness, weariness, guilt, change, illness, provision, family, and more.
• A 21-day path — "Resting in the Sovereignty of God," a short daily reading
  with a reflection, prayer, and one thing to practice.
• Save the words that steady you, and return to them anytime.

FULLY OFFLINE & PRIVATE
Everything lives on your device. No account, no sign-up, no internet required,
no tracking, and no ads. What you save stays private to you.

A NOTE ON THE SOURCES
Scripture is quoted from the King James Version (public domain). The reflections
draw on the theme of God's sovereignty as expressed by A. W. Pink in his
public-domain work "The Sovereignty of God," woven together with original,
accessible devotional writing.

Whether you have five minutes or one hard moment, A Steady Word is here to point
you back to the God who holds it all.
```

## Content rating questionnaire (answers)

Run Play Console's rating questionnaire and answer honestly — expected outcome
is **Everyone / PEGI 3**:
- Violence, sexual content, profanity, controlled substances, gambling: **No** to all.
- User-generated content / social features / user interaction: **No.**
- Shares location / personal info: **No.**
- References to religion are fine and do not raise the rating.

## Data safety form (answers)

- **Does your app collect or share any user data?** → **No.**
- (Everything the app stores — saved passages, plan progress — stays on the
  device and is not collected by you or sent anywhere, so it is not "collected"
  in Google's sense.)
- **Is all data encrypted in transit?** → Not applicable (no data leaves the device).
- **Do you provide a way to request data deletion?** → Users can clear app data /
  uninstall; nothing is held server-side.
